Four core scenarios

Built around everyday hospital work

Modular capabilities can be selected and combined according to hospital workflow, budget and deployment needs.

01

Outpatient records

Generates structured outpatient records from clinical conversations, supports pre-consultation data, real-time quality checks and common-disease decision assistance.

  • Voice-to-record generation
  • Real-time outpatient quality control
  • Clinical decision assistance
02

Inpatient records

Supports documentation throughout the admission cycle, including first progress notes, daily notes and discharge records.

  • Full-cycle document generation
  • Medical terminology and logic refinement
  • Condition and treatment assistance
03

Medical record quality control

Checks outpatient, emergency and inpatient records for completeness, consistency, logic, compliance and clinical appropriateness.

  • Deep content quality control
  • Configurable natural-language rules
  • Multi-dimensional quality dashboards
04

Health screening

Combines examination data into structured health reports and links imaging findings into an integrated risk view.

  • AI health examination report
  • One-scan multi-condition screening
  • Integrated risk interpretation
