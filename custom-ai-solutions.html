Selected use cases

AI outcomes, made visible

The same development framework can be adapted to different industries, data types and operating environments.

CASE 01
Multimodal content automation

AI digital human livestream

A configurable virtual presenter can combine speech synthesis, language models, visual generation and broadcasting controls to deliver consistent live content. The solution can be adapted for commerce, product explanation, training or service briefings while retaining human review and brand governance.

  • Multilingual scripts and natural speech generation
  • Configurable presenter, scene and knowledge base
  • Human approval, content controls and performance analytics
CASE 02
Computer vision at the edge

Road traffic licence plate recognition

A vision pipeline can detect vehicles, locate plates, recognise characters and connect results with authorised systems. Models can be tuned for local plate formats, camera positions, lighting and weather, supporting parking, logistics, access control and road-management workflows.

  • Vehicle detection, tracking and plate localisation
  • OCR tuned for local formats and operating conditions
  • Edge or private deployment with configurable data retention
